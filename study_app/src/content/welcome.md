## **Welcome to the study**

Thank you for taking part! If you've already completed the consent form and
baseline questionnaire from the invitation email, you're all set to begin —
you don't need to do anything else outside this app.

**This study is best completed on a laptop or desktop computer with a
full-size screen.** The task material and prompt editor are laid out side
by side and are hard to use on a phone or tablet.

### What happens next

1. A short introduction to Gherkin / BDD notation with examples
2. Prompt-writing guidelines
3. Two writing tasks — you'll write a prompt for an LLM to solve each problem

After you finish both tasks, I'll follow up separately with a short
post-study survey by email.

If you have any questions at any point, feel free to contact me at
[michal.okrasa@unine.ch](mailto:michal.okrasa@unine.ch).
