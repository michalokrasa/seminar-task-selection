## **You're ready to start**

The next step is the **first of four tasks**. You will be given a problem description and asked to write a specification that would let an LLM solve it; later you will write a second specification for the same problem in the other format.

Take your time — there is no time limit, and you can save a draft at any point before submitting.

Your progress is saved automatically as you go — including an **autosave
every 30 seconds** while you're writing a specification — so you can safely close
this tab and come back later; you'll resume exactly where you left off.

You will have access to the Gherkin intro and specification guidelines at all times.

**Remember not to use AI to write the specification for you. The goal of this study is to understand how you approach writing specifications, so using an LLM to do it for you would defeat the purpose.**

**Please use English when writing your specification.**

If you have any questions at any point, feel free to contact me at [michal.okrasa@unine.ch](mailto:michal.okrasa@unine.ch).
