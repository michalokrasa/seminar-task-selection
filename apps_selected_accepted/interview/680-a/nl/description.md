# Minimize Card Sum

## Goal

Determine the minimum possible sum of numbers on the remaining cards after optionally discarding up to three cards with the same number.

## Rules

- You have five cards, each with a positive integer.
- You can discard at most once either two or three cards with the same number.
- If no two or three cards have the same number, you cannot discard any cards.

## Input / Output

- **Input**: A single line containing five integers \( t_1, t_2, t_3, t_4, t_5 \) where \( 1 \leq t_i \leq 100 \).
- **Output**: A single integer representing the minimum possible sum of the numbers on the remaining cards.
