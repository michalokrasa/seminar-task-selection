Given exactly 5 cards each bearing a positive integer, you may optionally perform one discard operation: remove either 2 or 3 cards that all carry the same value. The operation can be performed at most once, and only if the required number of matching cards exists. Find the minimum possible sum of the cards that remain after the optimal discard. The algorithmic approach is brute-force enumeration over all values that appear 2 or 3+ times; the main challenge is correctly considering both group sizes and choosing the discard that maximises the reduction in sum.
