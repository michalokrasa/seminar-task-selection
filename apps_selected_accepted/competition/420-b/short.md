Given a partial chronological log of login/logout events in an online meeting, determine which participants could be the "leader" — defined as someone who is present at every moment when at least one person is online. Because only a fragment of the meeting is recorded, participants who never appear in the log are still candidates (their presence outside the fragment is unknown). Participants who logged off while others were still online, or were absent during a non-empty period, are disqualified. The algorithmic approach is event-driven simulation tracking the current online set; the main challenge is correctly inferring which participants are disqualified based on the partial log, particularly handling those who log off while the meeting is still active.
