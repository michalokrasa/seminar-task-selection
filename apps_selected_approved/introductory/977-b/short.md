A two-gram is any ordered pair of capital Latin letters. Given a string of n capital letters, find the two-gram (two consecutive characters) that appears most often as a substring, counting overlapping occurrences. Any two-gram with the maximum count is an acceptable answer. The algorithmic approach is a single linear scan with frequency counting over all n-1 consecutive pairs; the main challenge is correctly counting overlapping occurrences, though a simple dictionary or Counter handles this naturally.
