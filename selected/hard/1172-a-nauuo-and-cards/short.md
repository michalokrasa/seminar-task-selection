There are 2n cards: n numbered 1 to n, and n empty (0). Nauuo holds n of them; the rest form a pile. In one operation, she plays a card from her hand to the bottom of the pile and draws the top card. The goal is to arrange the pile so that card i is at position i (top to bottom) in the minimum number of operations. The solution requires greedily identifying how many numbered cards are already correctly positioned at the top of the pile as a prefix.
