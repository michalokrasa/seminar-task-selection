A boss has initial health `max` and regenerates `reg` HP per second. Each second: active spells deal damage, then the boss regenerates, then the player may use one scroll. A scroll with power `pow` can only be used when the boss is at or below `pow`% health, and deals `dmg` damage per second permanently. Determine if the boss can be defeated, and if so, find the minimum time and which scrolls to use greedily to maximize DPS as early as possible.
