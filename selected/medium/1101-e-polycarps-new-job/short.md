Process a stream of two query types: add a bill of size x×y to a collection, or check whether all bills collected so far fit into a wallet of size h×w. A bill fits in a wallet if it can be oriented (possibly rotated) to fit within the wallet's dimensions. The check reduces to tracking the max of each bill's larger dimension and the max of each bill's smaller dimension across all bills, then verifying both fit in the wallet in some orientation.
