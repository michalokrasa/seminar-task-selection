Anton wants to know if he performed well in a contest, defined as having outscored at least one participant whose handle was red (rating ≥ 2400 before the contest) and whose rating increased after the contest. Given the names, before-ratings, and after-ratings of n participants Anton outscored, output "YES" if any of them satisfies both conditions, "NO" otherwise. The problem is a simple linear scan with two conditions per participant.
