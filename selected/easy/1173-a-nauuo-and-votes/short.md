Given x confirmed upvotes, y confirmed downvotes, and z votes of unknown direction, determine the final result of a comment. The result is "+" if upvotes exceed downvotes, "-" if downvotes exceed upvotes, and "0" if they are equal. If the z unknown votes can lead to two different outcomes, print "?" to indicate the result is uncertain. The key insight is to check both extremes: all z votes going to upvotes and all going to downvotes.
